How to use the Foster & Kinship Companion

  1. Add a child. Tap Add a child and enter a name or initials. Add one for each child in your care — each gets its own checklist tab.
  2. Enter the placement date. Once you add the date the child was placed with you, the app shows Day X of 30 and counts up on its own each day — so you always know where you are in that first-month window. You don't update it; it refreshes automatically.
  3. Fill the can't-wait fields first. Allergies, who to call in an emergency, and who can consent to care.
  4. Work down the list. Check off what to ask, gather, and schedule. You can jot quick answers and dates right on each item.
  5. Add contacts & notes. Caseworker, doctor, pharmacy, and questions for the next visit.
  6. Print anytime. Tap Print / Save PDF for a clean binder or handoff page. Under Logs to keep going, print blank forms to photocopy.
  7. It saves itself. Everything stays on this device until you remove it — come back anytime, and reuse it for every new placement.
Removing a child: when a child moves on — or once you've printed their binder copy — scroll to the bottom of their checklist and tap Remove this child. Print first: it can't be undone.
