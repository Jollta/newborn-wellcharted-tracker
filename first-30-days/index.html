The First 30 Days After Placement

A new placement moves fast. Keep track of what to ask for, what to gather, and what to schedule — for one child or several — then print it clean for a binder or a handoff.

Nurse-built by Tammy, FNP-C · founder, WellCharted

Start here: Before anything else, write down any allergies and know who to call in an emergency. Add a child below and fill those two fields first — everything else you can gather as you go.

Everything you type stays saved on this device until you remove a child. Come back any time to keep going — and reuse this for every new placement by adding a new child.
